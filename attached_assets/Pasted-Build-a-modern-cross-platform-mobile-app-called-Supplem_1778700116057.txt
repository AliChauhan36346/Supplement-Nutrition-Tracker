Build a modern cross-platform mobile app called **Supplement Tracker Pro** for iOS and Android. The app should help users consistently take vitamins, supplements, protein, medicine, and wellness products through reminders, habit tracking, refill alerts, and progress insights. Design it as a polished subscription-ready app with strong retention, clean UX, and scalable architecture.

## Core Goal

Users often forget to take supplements or stop after a few days. This app solves consistency and adherence through smart reminders, streaks, education, and tracking.

---

## Target Audience

* Gym users taking protein / creatine / pre-workout
* General health users taking vitamins
* Elderly users taking daily supplements
* Busy professionals
* Users managing multiple supplements
* Parents managing family supplements

## Core Features (MVP)

### 1. Onboarding Flow

Ask:

* Goal: Energy / Fitness / Immunity / General Health / Weight Loss
* Age range
* Gender (optional)
* Number of supplements used
* Reminder preference
* Timezone auto-detect

Then personalize dashboard.

---

### 2. Add Supplement

Allow adding supplements manually or by search.

Fields:

* Supplement name
* Category (Vitamin, Mineral, Protein, Herb, Medication, Other)
* Dosage
* Units (mg, mcg, g, capsule, tablet, scoop, ml)
* Frequency:

  * Once daily
  * Twice daily
  * Custom days
  * Weekly
  * Monthly
* Time(s)
* With food / before food / after food
* Notes
* Start date
* End date optional

---

### 3. Barcode Scanner

Use camera to scan bottle barcode and auto-fill:

* Product name
* Brand
* Serving size

Fallback to manual input.

---

### 4. Smart Reminder System

Push notifications:

* Time to take Vitamin D
* Time for Creatine
* Refill needed soon

Actions inside notification:

* Mark Taken
* Snooze 10 min
* Skip

Reminder logic:

* If user ignores repeatedly, optimize future reminder timing.

---

### 5. Daily Dashboard

Show:

* Today’s supplement schedule
* Pending doses
* Completed doses
* Current streak
* Adherence % this week
* Quick tap “Taken”

Use cards + timeline UI.

---

### 6. History & Calendar

Calendar showing:

* Green = completed
* Yellow = partial
* Red = missed

Tap date to see details.

---

### 7. Streak & Gamification

* Daily streak counter
* Weekly consistency badge
* 30-day challenge
* Milestones
* XP points

---

### 8. Refill Tracker

Each supplement stores bottle quantity.

Example:
60 capsules / 2 daily = refill alert in 30 days.

Send alerts 7 days before running out.

---

### 9. Insights & Analytics

Charts for:

* Adherence %
* Missed doses
* Best consistency time
* Monthly streaks

Recommendations:
“You take supplements more consistently at 8am.”

---

### 10. Family Mode

Allow managing supplements for:

* Parents
* Children
* Partner

Separate profiles.
