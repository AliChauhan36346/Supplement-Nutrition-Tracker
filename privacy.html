Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 15, 2026

Overview

VitaRoutine ("the App", formerly known as Supplement Tracker Pro) helps you track vitamins, supplements, and wellness products daily. This privacy policy explains what data we store, how it is handled, and your choices regarding your privacy.

Data We Store

By default, your profile details (name, goal, age range), supplement list, dose history, streaks, and tracking preferences are stored locally on your device using secure on-device storage. We do not operate a cloud account system in this version, meaning your personal data does not leave your device unless you actively choose features that require network communication.

Network Features & Third Parties

Barcode Lookup: If you use the barcode scanner, the app may send the scanned barcode number to Open Food Facts (a public third-party open database) to retrieve the product name, category, and brand. This request does not transmit any personally identifiable information or health profile data.

Camera Access: Camera permissions are required only to scan product barcodes. The camera feed is processed entirely on your device, and no video feed or images are captured or uploaded to any server.

Notifications

If you grant permission, the App schedules local reminders on your device to help you take your supplements on time. All reminder content, scheduling, and triggers stay entirely on-device and are not shared with us or any third parties.

Analytics & Advertising

This app does not contain any third-party analytics (like Google Analytics or Mixpanel), advertising SDKs (like AdMob), or marketing tracking pixels. Your tracking habit remains completely private to you.

Your Rights & Controls

You have full control over your data. You can delete all local data at any time from Profile → Reset All Data. You can also revoke camera or notification permissions at any time through your device's native settings.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect or solicit personal information from children. Please do not use the App to store sensitive health information for a child without appropriate parental or legal guardian guidance.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time as features evolve (for example, if cloud backup or online accounts are introduced). Material changes will be reflected by an updated date at the top of this page and highlighted in the app's settings menu.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or your data, please contact the developer through the Google Play Store or Apple App Store listing support channels.
